Churn rate, also known as customer churn, is the rate at which a customer suspends a transaction with a company. Most commonly, it is expressed as the percentage of service subscribers who canceled their subscription within a certain time period. In this project, We aim to deploy a model which predicts the likelihood of a customer to churn in the near future or not. We will use Amazon Web Services(AWS) to build, train and deploy the model to predict the outcome.
